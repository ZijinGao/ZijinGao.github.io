So why food? Before we get down to business,
I want to spend a little time explaining to you
why I chose food as tonight's theme.

For a long time in history,
being able to feed oneself has been the ultimate goal for human being.
However, as time goes by, people became more powerful
and consequently they desire more than just being full --
they wanted to enjoy what they ate.

From then on, food as been developed rapidly into various styles and flavors.
Now, food had surpassed the gap between cultures and geographical regions,
and become an expression of people's lifestyle.
This is why food becomes such an important and interesting topic for now.
Because what you eat, to some extent, says who you are.
