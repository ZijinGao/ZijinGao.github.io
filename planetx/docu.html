Project Description
In the form of a variation of Conway's Game of Life, this game aims to explore the possibility in the future, when technology has taken over every aspect of human's life and people have to try hard to maintain the balance of modern technology and the nature which they originally come from.

At the start of the game, user will be given a "blank" (actually black but has no elements on it) world where they can choose to put nuclear stations,forests, and water areas onto the landscape and build their world.

These elements all have different properties. The nuclear stations generate the necessary nuclaer energy to the world, while they also accumulate nuclear waste which will pollute the areas near them; The forests and water areas respectively generate natural energy and brings up the sanitation level of the world, yet planting too many of them will leave not enough space for the nuclear stations to be placed. The eventual goal will be to evolve human being, and the only way to achieve this is to reach and keep the balance between natural energy, nuclear energy and sanitation level.
